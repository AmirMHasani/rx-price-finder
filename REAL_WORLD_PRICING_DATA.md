## Metformin 500mg

### Cash Prices (Without Insurance)
**Source: GoodRx, SingleCare, Drugs.com**

- **30 tablets:** $7.06 (Drugs.com average)
- **60 tablets:** $10-$30 (GoodRx/SingleCare range), $14.90 (GoodRx specific)

**Normalized to 30 tablets:**
- Low: $3.53 (half of $7.06)
- Mid: $7.45 (half of $14.90)
- High: $15.00 (half of $30)

### GoodRx Coupon Prices (30 tablets)
- **Hy-Vee:** $2.00 (with GoodRx)

### Medicare Part D
- **Coverage:** Yes, metformin is covered by Medicare Part D
- **Tier:** Tier 1 (preferred generic)
- **Typical Copay:** $0-$10 for Tier 1 generics
- **2025 Deductible:** Up to $590 maximum
